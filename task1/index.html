About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation (IAF) is an Indian non-profit organization established on 23 September 2020 by Govind Shukla. The organization is registered as a Section 8 Company under Indian law and is headquartered in Bilaspur. It works across multiple states in India with a mission to promote social welfare, education, women’s empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and employability development.

Legal Status & Certifications

According to the organization, InAmigos Foundation holds the following registrations and certifications:

  1. Section 8 Registered Non-Profit Organization
  2. 80G & 12A Certification (tax benefits for eligible donors)
  3. CSR-1 Registration (eligible for Corporate CSR partnerships)
  4. NITI Aayog NGO Darpan Registration
  5. ISO 9001:2015 Certification

Our Mission

To create sustainable social impact by empowering communities through education, skill development, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and humanitarian support initiatives.

Major Projects

Project SEVA

Provides food distribution, clothing support, blood donation drives, and humanitarian assistance to underprivileged communities.

Project Bachpanshala

Focuses on education for underprivileged children through academic support, digital literacy, and life skills training.

Project Udaan

Promotes women’s empowerment through skill development, entrepreneurship training, leadership programs, and menstrual hygiene awareness.

Project Jeev

Dedicated to animal welfare through feeding, rescue operations, care initiatives, and awareness campaigns.

Project Prakriti

Supports environmental sustainability through tree plantation drives and environmental awareness programs.

Project Vikas

Enhances employability through internships, career guidance, professional training, and skill development programs.
